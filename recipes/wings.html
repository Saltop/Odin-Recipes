Buffalo Wings

Description

A classic in American foods. The Buffalo wing is not only consumed all across america but all across the world itself. This page will show everything needed to prepare your own american classic including a shopping list.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Flour
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Buffalo Sauce of Choice
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Dry your wings with a paper towel
  2. In 2 seperate bowls add your flour and eggs make sure your eggs are whisked well
  3. Heat up your oil until it starts to crackle
  4. Roll wings in flour bowl, transfer into the egg bowl and then roll them in the flour again
  5. Add wings to oil until they turn a crispy golden brown
  6. Remove wings from oil carefully and set aside until all wings are finished
  7. Once all wings are finished place them into a large lidded container and add a generous amount of buffalo sauce (or your sauce of choice)
  8. Shake the wings in this container until it looks like they are all well coated
  9. Enjoy :)
