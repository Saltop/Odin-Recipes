Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and Cheese is known across the world as a comfort food unrivaled. It can be executed in as many ways as there are stars in the sky. The type of mac and cheese you make reflects onto you as a person. Spicy? Smooth? Bold and exciting? There are no limits when it comes to mac and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Noodle of your choice. (Shells or Macaroni highly recommended
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Shredded Mozzarella

Steps

  1. In a large pot begin to boil enough water to submerge your noodles
  2. in a seperate pot add a touch of butter, flour, and Milk
  3. Combine these over low to medium heat adding more milk or flour to achieve a thick sauce consistency
  4. Add your cheese mix to the flour, butter, and milk mixture. keep stirring until desired consitency and falvor is achieved. Dont be afraid to play around with the mixture
  5. Once your water begins to boil add your noodles to it
  6. let them cook until they achieve the consistency you want (around 10-15 minutes depending)
  7. Strain out the remaining water from your noodles
  8. Making sure all the water is gone readd your noodles to the pot and then pour your cheese sauce over top. Mix it all together and enjoy!
