Burgers

Description

The true american classic. Known around the world as the most american food there is. A staple in many bistros and restaurants across the country. Burgers are varied and expressionful. With an abundance of toppings and ways to make it your own.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Season your ground beef with salt and pepper as well as any other seasonings you like.
  2. Form your ground beef into appropiately sized patties for your buns. (Remember they will shrink a little bit)
  3. Over Med-High heat begin to cook your burgers for approximately 6 minuntes per side
  4. While your burgers are cooking chop up your onions into rings and your pickles into small slices
  5. Once burgers are done set it on top of your bun, followed by lettuce, onions, pickles, and then any other toppings you might like that may not be on this list, such as tomato or mushroom.
  6. Enjoy
